# CackOps — Pricing FAQ

## How long does setup take?
Most setups are done in 30–90 minutes depending on current state.

## What if I already installed OpenClaw?
Great. We start with what you have and harden from there.

## Do I need to be technical?
No. Guided and DFY options are built for non-expert operators.

## What if something breaks after setup?
You can add stabilization support or move to Managed Bot Ops.

## Is this only for Telegram?
Telegram is the fastest path. Additional channels can be added based on your use case.

## What do you need from me?
Basic intake info + access needed for setup (depending on tier).

## Which tier should I choose?
- Choose **Guided** if you want to learn and save cost.
- Choose **DFY** if you want fastest result with least effort.
- Choose **Managed** if you want ongoing reliability and improvements.
